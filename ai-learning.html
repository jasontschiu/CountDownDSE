How to Learn with AI

如何使用AI學習

01

Start with a clear task

Ask AI to teach, not just answer.

Instead of asking for the final answer, ask AI to explain the idea, show the steps, and give you one similar question to try.

Explain this topic in simple language, then give me one practice question. Do not give the answer until I try.
02

Check your understanding

Use AI as a patient tutor.

After studying, explain the concept back to AI. Ask it to find gaps in your explanation and correct only the weak parts.

I will explain this in my own words. Tell me what is correct, what is missing, and one thing I should revise.
03

Revise actively

Turn notes into practice.

Good revision is not rereading. Ask AI to turn your notes into short quizzes, flashcards, and exam-style questions.

Based on these notes, create 5 quiz questions from easy to hard. Ask one question at a time and wait for my answer.
04

Stay honest

Let AI coach your thinking.

If AI writes everything for you, you feel faster but learn less. Use it to question your reasoning and improve your next attempt.

Do not rewrite my full answer. Give me three specific suggestions so I can improve it myself.
